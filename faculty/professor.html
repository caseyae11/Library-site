Story 1 (faculty)

Professor Zhou attended a technology and education (TESS) Conference in November. She was excited to hear all about Open Educational Resources (OER) from presentations by eCampusOntario and other speakers. At the next faculty meeting, Professor Zhou shares all the amazing things she learned about OER. Her colleagues are all eager to hear about OER and other free alternatives to commercial textbooks, and the timing is perfect since the faculty is developing a new data science program. They're keen on saving students money and being able to customize content from different resources. Professor Zhou offers to do some research and report back to her colleagues and Associate Dean.
